Hi! I am a PhD Candidate at the [National Technical University of Athens](https://www.ntua.gr/en/) and a PhD Fellow at the [Archimedes Research Unit](https://archimedesai.gr/en/) of [Athena Research Center](https://www.athenarc.gr/en/home), advised by Prof. [Kostas Daniilidis](https://www.cis.upenn.edu/~kostas/) and Prof. [Petros Maragos](https://robotics.ntua.gr/members/maragos/). My research interests lie in Computer Vision and Machine Learning, with a particular focus on geometric computer vision, 3D understanding, neural rendering, and language-guided visual learning. I have also been a Visiting Associate at the [GRASP Lab](https://grasp.upenn.edu/) at the University of Pennsylvania.

Before starting my PhD, I completed my MSc in Artificial Intelligence at the [University of Amsterdam](https://www.uva.nl/en), where my thesis on language-driven 3D scene understanding was supervised by Prof. [Martin R. Oswald](https://oswaldm.github.io/) and Dr. [Silvan Weder](https://www.silvanweder.com/). Prior to that, I completed my undergraduate studies in Electrical and Computer Engineering at the National Technical University of Athens, where I was supervised by Prof. [Petros Maragos](https://robotics.ntua.gr/members/maragos/) during my thesis.
